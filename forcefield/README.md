The tabGAP potential used for comparison in this work was obtained directly from its original source, as published by Zhao et al. (Zhao, J. et al., npj Comput. Mater. 9, 159 (2023), https://doi.org/10.1038/s41524-023-01117-1). We utilized the potential in its published form without any modifications or retraining. This ensures that our benchmark accurately reflects the performance of the original tabGAP model as it has been applied in previous studies. Our implementation follows the input parameters and simulation protocols described in the original publication to guarantee a fair and reproducible comparison with our newly developed NEP model.
